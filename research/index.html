Questions I am tracking

What does good communication look like in dementia-related care?

Beyond giving information, good communication should reduce uncertainty, preserve agency, support family decision-making, and avoid making patients feel like a clinical object with a calendar.

How can nursing research become more accessible?

I am interested in summaries, visual explanations, bilingual communication, and public-facing translation of evidence.

Which community interventions can support cognitive health?

This includes exercise, social engagement, health education, caregiver support, and culturally suitable community programmes.

How do cultural backgrounds shape health decisions?

Healthcare is never delivered into a vacuum. Beliefs, family structure, language, trust, and social identity shape how people hear medical advice.
