HKU Nursing · Hosting · Professional communication

Professional hosting at the edge of nursing, ageing, and global health dialogue.

I served in a hosting / student ambassador capacity during the HKU School of Nursing international forum and anniversary events, supporting a setting where nursing leadership, proactive ageing, and cross-disciplinary healthcare were the main conversation.

My role

This was a professional communication setting, not a casual school activity. The work involved stage presence, bilingual awareness, event flow, guest-facing communication, and composure while many moving parts had to stay aligned.

Official context

HKU School of Nursing describes the 15th Hong Kong International Nursing Forum cum International Forum on Proactive Health and Ageing as part of its 30th Anniversary moment. The official overview states that the forum aimed to promote interdisciplinary development in nursing, facilitate collaboration among nurses, healthcare professionals and policymakers, and strengthen international cooperation in nursing and health system transformation.
