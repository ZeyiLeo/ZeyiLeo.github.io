HKU · Admissions · Student ambassador

Helping prospective students understand HKU in clear, useful language.

As a student ambassador, I support admissions-facing communication and HKUMed outreach by translating student experience, programme information, and campus life into conversations that feel usable.

RoleHKU Student Ambassador / HKUMed social student ambassador
SettingHKU Information Day and admissions outreach
AudienceProspective students and families
Core skillStudent-facing explanation and trust building

What this work involves

Student ambassador work is not only representation. The practical skill is listening quickly, understanding what the visitor is really asking, and giving an answer that is clear, honest, and matched to their background.

Official context

HKU’s official admissions page lists HKU Information Day 2025 for Undergraduate Admissions as a campus-wide event on 25 October 2025, 09:00–18:00. HKUMed’s Info Day page lists admissions talks, consultations, programme information, game booths, and guided tour activities across the medical campus, including Bachelor of Nursing and Bachelor of Nursing Advanced Leadership Track sessions.

What it contributes to my profile

This is applied communication: explaining institutions, programmes, and choices to real people under time pressure. It also links directly to my broader interest in making complex systems easier to enter.
