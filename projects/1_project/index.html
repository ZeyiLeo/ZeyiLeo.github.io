A programme ran by HKFDA to educate young directors

🌟 “Redemption of The General” Director: Zhu Zhili Producers: Zhuang Wenqiang, Huang Jin, Zheng Baorui Starring: Jiang Dawei, Liu Jiang Shanghai Peking Opera actor Zheng Bo collapsed while performing “Redemption of The General” and was later diagnosed with advanced throat cancer. In his youth, Zheng Bo had conflicts with his junior brother Yuan Kuan and accidentally blinded Yuan Kuan in one eye with a spear. Later, Yuan Kuan went to Hong Kong, and Zheng Bo lived with regret for this incident throughout his life. In the final moments of his life, Zheng Bo finally decides to go to Hong Kong to seek forgiveness from Yuan Kuan… Trailer

🌟 “No Way Out” Director: Huang Jiahao Producers: Zhuang Wenqiang, Huang Jin, Zheng Baorui Starring: Leung Wing-ting, Lu Junguang, Chen Zhanwen, Li Min A law firm goes bankrupt, and prospective owner Ah Lo urgently needs cash flow. Taking a risk, he robs a buyer of virtual currency. Office clerk Ah-Yo’s home is occupied by intruders, and feeling her life is threatened, she decides to emigrate abroad by misappropriating public funds. The destinies of two unrelated individuals intertwine, but who can remain untainted in this tumultuous era? Trailer

🌟 “Blindfold” Director: Leung Ho-lun Starring: Cheung Siu-hong Introverted and obedient Kai-leung moves to a new home with his superstitious mother. Feeling alienated in the new environment, he gradually becomes curious about the vision of paradise under the guidance of his class monitor and yearns to reunite with his father there someday. However, he gradually discovers the evil beneath the vision—pious classmates demonize him to bully him, and a teacher who speaks of beautiful ideals coerces students’ beliefs. These phenomena make him resentful towards Catholicism, and he plans to retaliate using the religion his mother follows.

🎬📽🎞 HONG KONG FILM DIRECTORS’ GUILD MASTERCLASS IN FILM DIRECTING SHOWCASE

HUMAN TO HUMAN

Post-screening Discussion Guests: Soo Wing-ka, Lau Kwok-cheung, Yuen Fu-wah, Leung Wing-ting, Lee Po-chi

🎊 A Question

After arranging their father’s funeral, the three sisters of the Chau family set out to find Mrs. Mun, the “Question Lady”. Their journey is fueled by the middle sister’s desire to ask their father one question…

🎊 Love on the Rocks Facing marital crisis, Fung and Yan are kidnapped together on the day of their divorce. The kidnappers demand a ransom of 5 million, threatening their lives. Despite their differences, the couple must temporarily set aside their grievances and work together to escape captivity within 12 hours.

🎊 Do Not Resuscitate

With terminal cancer, Wei-cheng (played by Yuen Fu-wah) faces end-of-life decisions. His daughter, Sin-yuet (played by Leung Wing-ting), desperately tries to prolong his life, with Nurse Lui (played by Hui Tin-yau) taking over his care. Sin-yuet is torn between her love for her father and witnessing his suffering, forced to make a difficult choice.

The three short films showcased were all centered around the theme “Human to Human,” marking their first and only public screening. The subject matter was deeply moving, and each short film delicately portrayed emotions. After the screening, I had the incredible opportunity to meet the three directors – it was such an amazing experience! Everyone was incredibly warm and welcoming. My only regret was not being able to ask questions in Cantonese.
