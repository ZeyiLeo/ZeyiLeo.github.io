## Overview

The U21 HSG IPE Student Challenge connects students from different disciplines and regions to work on global health challenges.

## What I focus on

- Interprofessional communication
- Global health problem framing
- Team coordination across disciplines and cultures
- Translating different perspectives into a shared project direction

## Why it fits my direction

I am interested in becoming a bridge between people, fields, and systems. This challenge reflects that direction clearly: healthcare problems rarely belong to one discipline alone, because apparently human problems enjoy being inconveniently complex.
