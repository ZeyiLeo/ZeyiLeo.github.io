## Project frame

The challenge fits my long-term direction because healthcare problems rarely sit neatly inside one discipline. Nursing, medicine, public health, policy, communication, and culture all interfere with each other, as if reality has never heard of course categories.

My focus is on synthesising different disciplinary perspectives, asking clearer questions, and helping the team move from discussion to a coherent argument, recommendation, and final output.

## Official context

U21 Health Sciences describes the IPE International Student Challenge as an annual activity hosted by the Interprofessional Education Group since 2021. Public U21 HSG updates for the 2026 challenge describe a May 12 kickoff, 60+ students across the network, 8 interdisciplinary groups, and the theme of adaptive intelligence for inclusive and sustainable healthcare practice.

## What this develops

This project is less about adding another title and more about practicing a specific skill: making interdisciplinary teamwork produce something coherent. Collaboration works best when different perspectives are paired with structure.
