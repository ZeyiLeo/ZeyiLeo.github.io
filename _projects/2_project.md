## Overview

The Orbis Future Vision Leader Campaign gave me the opportunity to work on eye health awareness and public health education.

## Focus areas

- Glaucoma awareness
- Prevention-focused public education
- Outreach activity design
- Communication with children, elderly groups, and community partners
- Translating medical knowledge into accessible messages

## Reflection

This project strengthened my interest in health communication. A public health campaign is not just about information delivery. It is about making people care, understand, and act.
