## Overview

This project represents my ongoing development in healthcare research, especially around ageing, cognitive health, and nursing care.

## What I am building

- Academic reading and evidence-based thinking
- Research coordination and documentation skills
- Understanding of older adult care and cognitive impairment
- Clear communication of research ideas for different audiences

## Why it matters

Healthcare research only creates impact when it can be understood, discussed, and translated into practice. My research interest is therefore not only about collecting evidence, but also about communicating it clearly.
