## Official context

HKU School of Nursing describes the 15th Hong Kong International Nursing Forum cum International Forum on Proactive Health and Ageing as part of its 30th Anniversary moment. The official overview states that the forum aimed to promote interdisciplinary development in nursing, facilitate collaboration among nurses, healthcare professionals and policymakers, and strengthen international cooperation in nursing and health system transformation.
