<div class="leo-os-grid">
  <div class="leo-os-item"><span>01</span><h3>Understand the system</h3><p>Clinical problems are rarely only clinical. They involve behaviour, language, incentives, culture, and trust.</p></div>
  <div class="leo-os-item"><span>02</span><h3>Translate the evidence</h3><p>I focus on making medical, social, and research ideas usable for patients, families, teams, and public audiences.</p></div>
  <div class="leo-os-item"><span>03</span><h3>Build visible work</h3><p>Campaigns, talks, project pages, research summaries, outreach materials, and digital products turn thinking into something other people can use.</p></div>
