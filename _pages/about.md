## About

I am ZY LEE, also known as Leo, a healthcare student at the Li Ka Shing Faculty of Medicine, The University of Hong Kong. My interests sit between healthcare, communication, research, and human-centered innovation.

I care about what happens after knowledge is produced: how medical evidence is explained, how public health messages reach real communities, how teams from different disciplines understand each other, and how good ideas become visible work instead of staying trapped in meeting notes, where many ideas go to die quietly.
