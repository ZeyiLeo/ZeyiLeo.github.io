## Research direction

My current research interests center on ageing, cognitive health, dementia-related care, and community-based health support for older adults.

I am particularly interested in questions such as:

<div class="leo-card-stack">
  <div class="leo-card">
    <h3>How can nursing care better support older adults with cognitive impairment?</h3>
    <p>This includes dementia care, communication with older patients, family support, and practical interventions in clinical and community settings.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="leo-card">
    <h3>How can health information be communicated more clearly?</h3>
    <p>Many healthcare problems are also communication problems. I am interested in how evidence can be translated into messages that patients, families, and communities can use.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="leo-card">
    <h3>How can interdisciplinary teams create better health outcomes?</h3>
    <p>Through global health and interprofessional projects, I am exploring how students and professionals from different fields can work together on real health challenges.</p>
  </div>
</div>
