## Questions I am tracking

<div class="leo-question-grid">
  <details open>
    <summary>What does good communication look like in dementia-related care?</summary>
    <p>Beyond giving information, good communication should reduce uncertainty, preserve agency, support family decision-making, and avoid making patients feel like a clinical object with a calendar.</p>
  </details>
  <details>
    <summary>How can nursing research become more accessible?</summary>
    <p>I am interested in summaries, visual explanations, bilingual communication, and public-facing translation of evidence.</p>
  </details>
  <details>
    <summary>Which community interventions can support cognitive health?</summary>
    <p>This includes exercise, social engagement, health education, caregiver support, and culturally suitable community programmes.</p>
  </details>
  <details>
    <summary>How do cultural backgrounds shape health decisions?</summary>
    <p>Healthcare is never delivered into a vacuum. Beliefs, family structure, language, trust, and social identity shape how people hear medical advice.</p>
  </details>
</div>
