HKU Taster, which was first launched in 2014, aims to offer secondary students an exciting taste of the unique energy of HKU campus life. Students have the chance to learn from HKU's friendly and knowledgeable professors and experience a range of interesting workshops.

---
I participated in HKU taster seminar series for overseas students from Jan 22 to Jan 26 and  gave a script review speech at the last day.Then I got my certificate from HKU.

---

During the days ,we were devided into 10 differet groups inculding 10 people each.I studyed in different faculties like faculty of law and faculty of art.There is a varity of activities for us to take part in.
